Demo cue card

For you in the room — not the product tutorial.

Panic reset

  • Breathe. You are the guide, not the quant.
  • Point at the screen. Let the chart talk first.
  • You don’t derive the math. Verification is the audit trail.

Say this first

“BTC options research cockpit — not advice. I’ll show what the market prices in, where a view might differ, and how payoff fits. Five minutes.”

Do this (mechanical)

  1. Sidebar → Bitcoin on → Refresh priced inputs (Deribit) once.
  2. Scroll to Bitcoin implied lab → pick an Expiry.
  3. Click one preset → open Belief vs market → read one sentence aloud.

Skip Polymarket, forward curve, tickets unless they ask.

While the chart is up

“Purple and orange = what today’s option prices imply for this expiry. Not a forecast — a market readout.”

If they ask how it’s calculated

“Live Deribit chain turned into a density shape. Orange is from the chain; purple is a simpler baseline. The trust strip and Verification show provenance — I don’t derive it by hand on a call.”

Ask them (takes pressure off you)

“What was the main thing on screen — in your words?”

Then: “Would you open this before your next BTC options trade?”

Never say

  • Guaranteed profit / proven edge
  • “Recommended trade” from disagreement alone
  • Improvised formulas or greek lectures

After they leave

Log one row in validation reality checks. Comprehension Y/N · Return Y/N · one-line note.
